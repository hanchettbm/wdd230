    in as well as local information about trails and maps. This website will
    be able to tell them, weather information, provide articels about hikes
    common weather trends, as well as give great ideas about safe locations.
  • 2) A website manager for Weather4You check in. He works with the company,
    and requests site information. He asks us to add a drop down navigation
    bar and to update the articles with more current events for users.
  • 3) An Instagram influencer is in town. They plan to do a vlog in the
    city where Napoleon Dynamyte was filmed. They are looking for unique
    facts about the small town of Preston as well as info to prepare for
